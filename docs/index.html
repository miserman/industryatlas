About

This site is a rework of industryatlas.org.

It is built around the same County Business Patterns Database as of 2025-04, which was created as part of the Imputing Missing Values in the US Census Bureau's County Business Patterns paper.

This site was made by the Yale Data-Intensive Social Science Center.
